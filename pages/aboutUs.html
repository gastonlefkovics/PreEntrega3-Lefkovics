About Us

Allow me, dear friends, to introduce you to my handcrafted wooden guitars. Each one of them is unique, and is made with the most exquisite materials and the most refined techniques. I am a luthier and musical instrument maker who puts all his effort and passion into each piece that leaves his workshop. If you are looking for a guitar that is not only a musical instrument, but a true work of art, I invite you to get to know my creations.

Music is the most sublime of the arts, capable of touching our innermost being. I have always admired handcrafted wooden guitars, and the power they have to transmit emotions and feelings. As a luthier and builder of musical instruments, I have found in this art a way to express my love for music and my passion for craftsmanship. Every time I immerse myself in the creation of a new guitar, I feel an indescribable emotion, which can only be described as a moment of grace.
